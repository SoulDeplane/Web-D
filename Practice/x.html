OPD

Description

The Outpatient Department (OPD) provides medical consultation, diagnosis, and treatment for patients who do not require hospitalization. It is the first point of contact between patients and doctors.

Timings

  • Monday - Saturday: 9:00 AM - 5:00 PM
  • Sunday: Closed (Emergency services available)

Available Specialties

  • General Medicine
  • Pediatrics
  • Orthopedics
  • Gynecology
  • Dermatology
  • ENT
  • Ophthalmology
  • Cardiology

Services Offered

  • Doctor consultations
  • Routine health check-ups
  • Follow-up visits
  • Minor procedures
  • Prescription and medical advice

Consultation Fees

  • General Physician: ₹300
  • Specialist Consultation: ₹500 - ₹800

Required Documents

  • OPD registration card
  • Previous medical reports (if any)
  • ID proof (optional)

Location

Ground Floor - OPD Block
