Emergency

Description

The Emergency Department provides immediate medical care for patients with serious injuries, sudden illnesses, or life-threatening conditions. Services are available 24/7 with trained emergency staff.

Timings

  • Monday - Sunday: 24 Hours
  • Emergency services available all days

Emergency Services

  • Accident and trauma care
  • Cardiac emergencies
  • Stroke management
  • Burn injuries
  • Poisoning and overdose treatment
  • Respiratory emergencies

Facilities Available

  • 24/7 ambulance service
  • Emergency operation theatre
  • ICU and ventilator support
  • On-call specialist doctors
  • Advanced life support systems

Charges

  • Emergency consultation: ₹800
  • Ambulance service: As per distance

Required Documents

  • Patient ID (if available)
  • Previous medical records (if any)

Location

Ground Floor = Emergency Block
