Graphic Era (Deemed To Be) University

  • Graphic Era (Deemed To Be) University is a private university located in Dehradun, Uttarakhand, India.

  • It was established in 1993 as a private engineering college and was granted deemed university status in 2008.



  • The university offers undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs in various fields including engineering, management, computer applications, and humanities.

  • It is known for its focus on research and innovation, and has collaborations with several international universities and organizations.
