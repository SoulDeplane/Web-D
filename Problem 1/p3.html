Welcome to Creadeau Solutions - Innovating Your Business Digitally

Credeau is a fintech technology company focused on transforming the digital lending ecosystem for banks, NBFCs, and financial institutions.
The company provides an AI-powered lending operating system that enables faster, smarter, and more secure credit decision-making.
Credeau's platform integrates advanced machine learning models, intelligent APIs, and automated workflows to streamline the entire loan lifecycle, from customer onboarding and data collection to risk evaluation and final disbursement.
By combining data intelligence, fraud detection, compliance monitoring, and real-time analytics, Credeau helps financial organizations reduce operational costs, minimize risk, and improve decision accuracy.
The solutions are designed to be scalable, secure, and compliant with regulatory standards, empowering lenders to deliver seamless digital experiences while maintaining trust, transparency, and efficiency.

Our Services

  • AI-Based Lending Platform Development - End-to-end digital lending solutions with automated credit decisioning and workflow management.

  • Credit Risk & Fraud Assessment - Advanced analytics and machine learning models for risk scoring, fraud detection, and compliance checks.

  • Banking & NBFC Integrations - Secure API integrations for account aggregation, identity verification, and financial data processing.

  • Data Intelligence & Analytics - Real-time insights, reporting dashboards, and explainable AI for smarter financial decisions.
